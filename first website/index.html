The 7 rules of a great Git commit message

  1. Seperate subject from body with a blank line
  2. Limit the subject like to 50 characters
  3. Capitalize the subject line
  4. Do not end subject line with a period
  5. Use the impreative mood in the subject line
  6. Wrap the body at 72 characters
  7. Use the body to explain what and why vs. how

Image Formats

  1. JPG - For handling large color palettes without exorbitantly increasing file size
  2. GIF - The go-to for simple animations but they're somewhat limited in terms of color palette
  3. PNG - Great for anything that is not a photo or animated & have opacity
  4. SVG - Vector-based graphics meaning it can scale up or down without loss of quality
